Read Silicon ID v1.1 by KdL (2020.01.09)
========================================

This is a simple tool to identify EPCS1, EPCS4, EPCS16 and EPCS64 integrated devices
in the 1chipMSX, Zemmix Neo, 8bits4ever SX-1, VTrucco SM-X, Altera DE0, Altera DE1 and other boards.
